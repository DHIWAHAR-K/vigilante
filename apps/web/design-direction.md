# Vigilante UI Redesign - Design Direction

## Concept Summary
1. **Composer as Command Desk**: Transform the centered composer into a sophisticated workspace instrument with subtle ambient lighting and elevated surface treatment
2. **Sidebar as Research Workspace**: Give the sidebar a more substantial architectural presence with refined active states and better information hierarchy
3. **Signature Visual Language**: Implement a "precision notch" motif - subtle geometric accents that appear in active states, dividers, and interactive elements
4. **Material Hierarchy**: Create distinct layers with warm surface separation, refined borders, and controlled elevation that feel sophisticated in both light and dark modes

## Signature Visual Motif: "Precision Notch"
A subtle geometric accent that appears as a thin, precise cut or notch in surfaces. This motif will:
- Appear in active conversation items as a thin amber accent line
- Show in dividers as a precise hairline
- Appear in focus states as a subtle border highlight
- Be used in the composer's ambient field as a refined edge treatment
- Appear in the sidebar header as a clean accent

This motif reinforces the brand identity of vigilance, research, and precision while remaining understated and elegant.
